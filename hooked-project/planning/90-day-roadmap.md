# Hooked 90-Day Roadmap

## Month 1: Foundation and Core Dating

### Week 1
- Project setup, environments, and architecture skeleton.
- Auth, account model, and consent logging.
- Analytics event schema draft.

### Week 2
- Profile service and onboarding flow.
- Swipe feed and match engine.
- Initial landing and signup pages.

### Week 3
- Free text chat for matched users.
- Report/block baseline.
- Basic moderation intake pipeline.

### Week 4
- Wallet ledger foundations.
- Token bundle checkout integration.
- Billing history and receipts.

## Month 2: Monetization and Creator Tools

### Week 5
- Paid media in chat (image/video).
- Balance checks and payment failure states.
- Transaction audit trails.

### Week 6
- Creator profiles, subscription tiers, and PPV content.
- Tips and gift transactions.
- Creator earnings dashboard v1.

### Week 7
- Age verification provider integration.
- Enforcement gates for adult/premium features.
- Verification retry/error UX.

### Week 8
- Moderation queue for AI + human review.
- Case actions and escalation rules.
- Admin safety dashboard v1.

## Month 3: Paid Calls, Hardening, Launch

### Week 9
- Per-minute private call integration.
- Pre-authorization hold and live timer UI.

### Week 10
- Session reconciliation jobs.
- Auto-end and refund/release logic.
- Payment and call observability alerts.

### Week 11
- Full QA pass on mobile and desktop.
- Performance tuning and abuse simulation.
- Launch playbooks and incident runbook.

### Week 12
- Staged rollout (1% -> 10% -> 100%).
- KPI monitoring and post-launch fixes.
- Backlog grooming for Phase 2.

## Release Gates
- Free chat stable and spam controls working.
- Paid media and call billing accuracy within tolerance.
- Age verification fully enforced.
- Moderation response workflows operational.
- No critical P0 defects in core user journeys.
